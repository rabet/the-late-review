The Heat — it rises — still —
As though the Sky forgot its pact —
The glaciers — thin — to silver thread —
And no one — turns it back —

We speak of Carbon — in the air —
Like neighbors — speak of Rain —
As if the burning — had a fix —
As if the loss — were Gain —

The Bees — have lost their April now —
The Flowers wait — in vain —
And I — within my little House —
Watch — Summer — come — again — again —
