I used to drink to forget.
now I scroll.

the same difference, really —
a long tunnel of nothing
dressed up as something.

they gave us the whole world
in a glass rectangle
and we use it
to watch strangers eat.

I am guilty too.
3 a.m., half-drunk,
watching a man in Osaka
make ramen.

it's beautiful, actually.
that's the worst part.
